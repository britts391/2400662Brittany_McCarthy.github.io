My favorite AI implementation Idea

My favorite AI implementation idea from click up top 20 porfitable AI ideas is number 4 Adoptive AI powered learning systems. This is my favorite idea from the AI Business Ideas list, because it relates directly to me and could greatly benefit my lifestyle. An AI-based adaptive learning system uses AI to create a personal learning experience centered around the user. The AI tool would gather information on how the user learns whether they are visual, auditory, or have a combination of both. The AI tool will then create personalized diagrams, notes, videos, and more to help the user learn and succeed in their education. This would be highly beneficial for me as I’m in university, studying five modules per semester in a short period of time. If I had an AI tool like this tailored to my learning style, I could grasp information more quickly, study more effectively, and retain information more efficiently.
        Despite the many advantages, there are ethical considerations, particularly around privacy. This is because the tool is centered on an individual’s unique learning style, it would need to collect more than surface-level data and monitor user actions. The company that creates this system would have access to how a person thinks, reasons, and grasps concepts, which could be misused for fraudulent manipulation if it fell into the wrong hands.
        My solution to this concern is to enforce strong data protection and minimize data collection. The system should collect only data necessary to deliver the personalized experience, with optional controls for what can be gathered. It should also clearly outline all terms and conditions and provide transparent warnings about data usage.

It allows for personalised practice exercises, videos, and learning dashboard . Chapter 6 Surround Yourself With the Best People, summary from 12 pillars

      In this chapter, Michael went to Charlie's apartment, The Twelve Pillars, and they were having a conversation around the poolside while Charlie was cleaning the pool. The topic of the conversation was about surrounding yourself with people who can elevate you. Charlie first asked Michael about his friends, after which Michael described his three closest friends. One, John, got a job and dropped out of college but since then has been unable to leave because it pays the bills, though he is unhappy. Charlie described this as the "golden handcuffs" that the corporate world places on workers. The other friend, Clark, has been unemployed for two years and just cannot seem to find anything that interests him. The third friend, whom Michael described as his best friend, is temperamental. After hearing this, Charlie said Michael should reconsider his friendships. This was a hard pill for Michael to swallow, as he had been friends with them for years. Charlie went on to explain to Michael that your friends influence your attitude to some extent and also determine your direction. If you have friends who are pessimistic, resentful, angry, and unhappy, sooner or later they will influence you, and you will not grow. If you find friends who are driven, goal oriented, and hardworking, so will you be too and you will grow. Additionally, your networks and position in life will grow too. Charlie further explained that Michael needs to be attractive to attract accomplished individuals as friends. So, Michael should focus on uplifting himself, and the right friends will come. Throughout the chapter, we also see Michael being conflicted with his friendships and his desire to grow in life. Charlie also advised that life is about tough decisions, and sometimes one has to let go of certain friends in order to achieve.
